Virtual Density Lab

Part I - Density of a Liquid

In this part, you will get to determine the identity of an unknown liquid, by calculating the density using experimental data. You will also get to practice using significant digit rules and see the differences in using dirrent pieces of equipment in the lab.

Equipment to be used:
  1. top-loading balance
  2. analytical balance
  3. graduated cylinder
  4. volumetric pipet

Part II - Density of a Solid

In this part, you will determine the identity of an unknow solid by measuring it density. Again, you should pay close attention to how the choice of equipment used affects the number of significant digits (precision) in your final result.

Techniques Used:
  1. Direct method
  2. Displacement Method
    • Graduated cylinder method (constant liquid volume)
    • Pycnometer method (constant total volume)
