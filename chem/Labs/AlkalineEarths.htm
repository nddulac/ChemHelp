The Alkaline Earth Metals and the Halogens

In this experiment, you will

  1. Test the solubilty of several alkaline earth metal cations when paired with a variety of anions
  2. Test the relative oxidizing power of the halogens (Cl2, Br2, and I2)
  3. Use the results of Part A to determine the cation in an unknown alkaline earth halide
  4. Use the results of Part B to determine the anion in an unknown alkaline earth halide
