Determination of a Chemical Formula

In this experiment, you will determin the full chemical formula of a hydrate of a chloride salt.

Equipment used:

  1. Crucible
  2. Bunsen Burner
  3. Ring Stand with Clay Triangle
  4. Wire Gauze (to place hot crucible on for cooling)
  5. Analytical Balance
