The Oxidation of Copper

The half reaction for the oxidation of copper is

Cu --> Cu+2 + 2 e-
Note that in the oxidation of copper, as in any oxidation process, electrons are given off. Thus, electrons always appear on the product side of an oxidation half reaction. The number of electrons freed in the oxidation half reaction must be used completely by the reduction half reaction. This will be valuable to know when trying to balance oxidation/reduction reaction equations.
