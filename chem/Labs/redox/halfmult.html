Cu + 2 AgNO3 --> Cu(NO3)2 + 2 Ag

Copper wire, bent into the shape of a star, is placed in the bottom of a crystalizing dish. Silver (I) nitrate solution is poured over the copper wire.

Start

After the reaction has been allowed to go for a while, silver metal crystalizes on the copper wire. Copper metal goes into solution making the solution blue.

Finish

Copper is a better reducing agent than silver. Were silver the better reducing agent, the reaction would run in the other dirrection.
