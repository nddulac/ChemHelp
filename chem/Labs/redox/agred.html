The Reduction of Silver

The half reaction for the reduction of silver is

Ag+ + e- --> Ag
Note that in the reduction of silver, as in any reduction process, electrons are used up. Thus, electrons always appear on the reactant side of a reduction half reaction. The number of electrons usedd in the reduction half reaction must be come from the oxidation half reaction. This will be valuable to know when trying to balance oxidation/reduction reaction equations.
