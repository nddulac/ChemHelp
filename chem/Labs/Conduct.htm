Virtual Conductometric Lab

In this experiment, you will titrate a solution of Ba(OH)2 with a solution of H2SO4. You will folow the titration by meauring the conductivity of the solution.

Equipment to be used:
  1. volumetric pipet
  2. buret
  3. conductivity meter
