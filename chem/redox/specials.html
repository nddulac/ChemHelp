Special Atoms

There are three atoms which are always given the same oxidation number when they are found in compounds (other than pure elemental compounds!) The three most common elements which fall into this catagory are shown in the table below.

H +1
O -2
F -1

These elements will be useful in finding the oxidation numbers of the other elements in a compound.
