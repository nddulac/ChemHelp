The Pauli Exclusion Principle says ___.
No two electrons in an atom can have the same set of four quantum numbers. Electrons will occupy separate orbitals within a subshell until the subshell is more than half-filled. Electrons will occupy the lowest energy orbitals available. No two electrons are alike.

The Auf Bau Principle says ___.
No two electrons in an atom can have the same set of four quantum numbers. Electrons will occupy separate orbitals within a subshell until the subshell is more than half-filled. Electrons will occupy the lowest energy orbitals available. No two electrons are alike.
